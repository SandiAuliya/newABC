ABOUT US

ABC Learning Center

ABC Learning Center is a company that opens IT courses for beginners and intermediate to advanced. This company was founded on March 12, 1995. Founded by a man who really likes IT and wants to share the knowledge he has. The company is located in Indonesia, the province of Bali, Denpasar city. It was founded in Bali because many immigrants came to Bali, from outside the city, outside the province, and even outside the country. The economy in Bali is fairly stable, because many business people come to Bali. Not a few teachers who are experienced in the IT field come to Bali. Therefore, he founded this company to unite people who are proficient in IT to share their knowledge with people who want to study IT.


Purpose ABC Learning Center

The purpose of establishing this place is so that people who like IT can develop more if they study together at the ABC Learning Center with experienced and qualified teachers. And so that Indonesia is not left behind by advanced technology out there. ABC Learning Center provides 4 courses at affordable prices and flexible times.
